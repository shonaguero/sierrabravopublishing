The Story

Songs Written at the Turn of the Millennium. Lost for Twenty-Five Years.

Twin Wolves is Shon Aguero and Marcus Blair, lifelong friends and songwriters from Heavener, Oklahoma, now residing in Columbia, Missouri. Between 1999 and 2001, in their early twenties, they wrote a collection of songs together. Then life got in the way. The recordings disappeared.

Two best friends wrote these songs when they were young men. They did not understand them then. They do now.

Twenty-five years later, the original demos were recently recovered. Rebuilt with new live instruments, new vocals, and modern production, the songs reveal something neither man anticipated: they speak more clearly to who Aguero and Blair have become than to who they were when they wrote them.

Spectral Hymns is the first of two companion EPs releasing this summer. The Psychic Twin follows July 13, 2026.
